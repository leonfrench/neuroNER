 transcription  factor  binding  sites  involved  in  regulating  the  differences  between  murine  rod  and  cone photoreceptor  populations  To  identify  highly  conserved  motifs  enriched  in  promoters  that  drive  expression  in  either  rod  or  cone photoreceptors  we  assembled  a  set  of  murine  rod  specific  cone specific  and  non  photoreceptor  background  promoter  sequences  These  sets  were  used  as  input  to  a  newly  devised  motif  discovery  algorithm  called  Iterative  Alignment  Modular  Motif  Selection  IAMMS Using  IAMMS we  predicted  motifs  that  may  putative sites  for  IL effectors  in  rods  and  RXR family  members  in  cones  that  can  explain  experimental  data  showing  changes  to  cell fate  by  activating  these  signaling  pathways  during  rod  cone development  Two  of  the  predicted  motifs  NRE and  ROP have  been  confirmed  experimentally  to  be  involved  in  cell specific  expression  patterns  We  provide  a  full
